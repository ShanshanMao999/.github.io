Research

I build multi-agent simulations to study how social hierarchies emerge, collapse, and rebuild under cooperation, ability heterogeneity, and information manipulation. I quantify hierarchy order using Trophic Incoherence and track dynamic interaction networks (speaker→listener) during collective tasks.
